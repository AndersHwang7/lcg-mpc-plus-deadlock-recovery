LCG-MPC+ 4-Way Interactive Visualizations

This folder contains standalone HTML Canvas simulations that compare four planners on identical maps, seeds, robot counts, and start-goal assignments.

Compared planners: WHCA best preregistered, IMPC-DR-lite, MPC-CBF-lite, and LCG-MPC+.

  1. 01_ring_cross_56robots_seed0_whca_best_preregistered_vs_impc_dr_lite_vs_mpc_cbf_lite_vs_lcg_mpc_plus
  2. 02_intersection_cross_72robots_seed7_whca_best_preregistered_vs_impc_dr_lite_vs_mpc_cbf_lite_vs_lcg_mpc_plus
  3. 03_bottleneck_swap_80robots_seed13_whca_best_preregistered_vs_impc_dr_lite_vs_mpc_cbf_lite_vs_lcg_mpc_plus
  4. 04_warehouse_aisles_90robots_seed21_whca_best_preregistered_vs_impc_dr_lite_vs_mpc_cbf_lite_vs_lcg_mpc_plus
  5. 05_corridor_opposing_60robots_seed42_whca_best_preregistered_vs_impc_dr_lite_vs_mpc_cbf_lite_vs_lcg_mpc_plus

Inside each file, press space to play or pause, use the left/right arrow keys to step through time, and use the checkboxes to toggle path, intent, and tail overlays.

These animations provide qualitative behavior views. Quantitative manuscript claims are based on the 1000-seed statistical tables and paper figures.
